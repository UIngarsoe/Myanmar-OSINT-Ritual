# 🇬🇧 English

A wealthy person measures assets.

A wise person measures possibilities.

Money by itself changes nothing.

Wisdom determines where money travels and what future it creates.

The smallest coin can become:

- Education
- Hope
- Opportunity
- Freedom
- A better future

Therefore,

**Real prosperity is measured not by ownership, but by transformation.**
