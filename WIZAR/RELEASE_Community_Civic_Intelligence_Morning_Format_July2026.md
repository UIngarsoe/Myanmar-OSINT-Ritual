### 🎯 What is This Tool?

**Community Civic Intelligence Morning Format** is a short, structured morning meeting ritual designed to:

- Build shared understanding of current realities
- Practice calm, evidence-based reflection
- Reduce emotional reactivity and Avijja (delusion)
- Strengthen collective wisdom through diverse voices and AI assistance
- Support informed decision-making at community and organizational levels

It is intentionally simple — suitable for small groups, classrooms, or organizational daily huddles.

---

### 📋 How to Use (Simple Format)

1. One facilitator (Wiseman / Senior member)  
2. 6 participants with mobile phones + preferred AI  
3. Each participant shares one compact paragraph on today’s situation  
4. Participants consult their AI and share one-paragraph analysis  
5. Facilitator compiles the 6 perspectives  
6. Facilitator adds a short Final Morning Reflection (Dharma-informed)  
7. Session ends with commitment and Sadhu

**Duration:** 45–60 minutes
