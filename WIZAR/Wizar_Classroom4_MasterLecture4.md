## 🔍 Forensic Analysis: Causes, Effects, and Methods

Students must apply the standard analytical toolset—Integrity ($Sila$), Focused Awareness ($Samadhi$), and Discernment ($Panna$)—to dissect the strategic mechanisms deployed by the Rich Man.

### 1. The Method: Institutionalized Leverage & Conditional Compliance
The Rich Man does not engage in a standard transaction or immediate bribery. Instead, he implements an exact **6-month behavioral probation cycle**. 
* **The Key Transfer:** The physical asset (the Prado keys) is handed over immediately to capture psychological compliance.
* **The Document Holding:** The structural ownership documents are legally withheld for exactly six months. This creates an unescapable feedback loop where U Lar Ba’s everyday comfort depends on executing the Rich Man's unspoken commands.

### 2. The Technique: Non-Verbal Command Vectors
The execution of the compromise relies entirely on an un-logged, non-verbal system parameter: **The Thumbs-Up Signal (လက်မထောင်ပြခြင်း)**.
* Because no words are spoken during the live auction, the legal risk is distributed away from the Rich Man. 
* The decision maker (U Lar Ba) assumes all physical liability by driving down the gavel with brute force ("တုံးကို အားရပါးရ ထုပေးမယ်") based entirely on a simple, silent visual confirmation cue.

### 3. The Kamma Mechanics (Action vs. Asset Expansion)
* **The Cause:** The strategic placement of a "surplus" vehicle (`Prado 4x4`) into the decision-making node of an auction infrastructure.
* **The Effect:** The Rich Man's capital velocity scales exponentially. Within a 6-month evaluation window, his fleet operations expand from a base line of 2 vehicles to a massive institutional fleet of **12 vehicles**.
