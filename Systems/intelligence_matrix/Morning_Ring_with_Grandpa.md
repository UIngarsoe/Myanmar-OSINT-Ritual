### ❓ What is Neighbourhood Watch? (International Standard)

**Neighbourhood Watch** is a proven, non-partisan community program that began in the United States in the 1960s and has been adopted worldwide (UK, Australia, Canada, and beyond).

**Core Principles:**
- Neighbours actively look out for one another.
- Focus on prevention, connection, and reducing isolation — especially for seniors.
- Regular check-ins, sharing useful information, and building trust.
- Citizen-led, voluntary, and collaborative with local authorities.
- Modern versions include mental wellbeing, disaster readiness, and community resilience.

The heart of it is simple: **Good neighbours make strong communities.**
